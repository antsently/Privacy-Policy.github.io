Weather App

Privacy Policy

This Weather App is designed for users aged 3 and above. It does not collect any personal information or data from its users.

Data Collection

This app does not collect any personal information or data from its users. It solely utilizes the location information (with user permission) to provide weather updates and forecasts.

Use of Information

The location information obtained from the user is used solely for the purpose of providing weather forecasts and current weather updates within the app. This information is not stored or shared externally.

Age Restriction

This app is intended for users aged 3 and above. If you are under the age of 3, please do not use this app without parental supervision.
